# Open Source Software (OSS) in ABB

Users are permitted to download and use **Open Source Software (OSS)** for purposes such as:

- Research and Development (R&D)
- Testing activities
- Application dependencies
- Other approved business requirements

## Approval Requirement

The installation and usage of OSS must be approved by the designated OSS authority before use.

### OSS Approval Portal

To initiate an OSS approval request, use:

- [SDIP Portal - OCC](https://abb.sharepoint.com/sites/SDIP/Portal/SitePages/OCC.aspx)

## Recommended Process

Before initiating an OSS approval request, users are encouraged to:

1. Contact their **Country License Manager (CLM)**.
2. Verify whether the OSS is already approved or available for use within ABB.
3. Obtain guidance on the appropriate approval process and licensing considerations.

## Compliance

- OSS installations should comply with ABB policies and procedures.
- Users must ensure that the required approvals are obtained before deploying OSS for business purposes.
- Proper OSS governance helps manage licensing, security, and compliance risks associated with open-source software.
