# Local Software – Third-Party Software

The end user must reach out to the Business IS Manager of their respective division for any local software requirement.

## Procurement Process

1. The Business IS Manager will obtain the necessary quotations from the Country License Manager (CLM).
2. The CLM will share approximately **2 to 3 competitive quotations** from IS-authorized software vendors.
3. The requestor must:
   - Review the quotations.
   - Coordinate with their Business SCM team for commercial negotiations.
   - Release the Purchase Order (PO) to the selected vendor.
4. IS will facilitate and support the software procurement process as required.

## MyIS Requirement

- All local software requests must also be initiated through **MyIS**.
- This ensures complete visibility and tracking of local software procurements across the organization.
- MyIS serves as a centralized platform for managing both global and local software requests.
